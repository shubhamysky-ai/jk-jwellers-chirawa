92.5 Sterling Silver

Guaranteed Purity & Gifting Options

♦

At JK Jewellers Chirawa, our silver collection is crafted in 92.5% pure Sterling Silver, ensuring long-lasting durability, shine, and high return value. We also feature high-purity (99.9%) fine silver coins and bars for family investments and festival gifting during Diwali, Dhanteras, and weddings.

  • 925 Stamp of Authenticity: Every sterling item carries the laser hallmark stamp.
  • Pooja Articles: Handcrafted silver thalis, bells, diyas, and silver deities (Lakshmi-Ganesh idols).
  • Traditional Payals & Bichiyas: Exquisite heavy silver anklets with detailed craftsmanship.
