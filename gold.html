22K & 18K Yellow Gold

BIS Hallmarking & Carat Verification

♦

At JK Jewellers Chirawa, we prioritize honesty above everything. India has made HUID (Hallmark Unique Identification) mandatory, and we have fully adopted this standard. All our gold ornaments bear the 3 hallmarks of purity:

  • BIS Standard Mark: Certifies that the gold has been tested by government licensed centers.
  • Purity Grade: Clearly marked as 22K916 (91.6% purity), 20K833 (83.3% purity), or 18K750 (75% purity).
  • HUID Number: A unique 6-digit alphanumeric code laser engraved on every single ornament for tracking authenticity.

Carat Analyzer Scanners

Caratmeter Transparency

We house advanced computerized XRF Carat Scanners in our showroom. You can test your old gold or verify the exact purity of any item you purchase from us in just 2 minutes, completely free of charge.
