Legacy & Values

Welcome to JK Jewellers Chirawa

♦

At JK Jewellers Chirawa, we believe that jewellery is not just a commodity; it is a memory, an asset, and a symbol of love passed down through generations. Founded with the mission to provide pure, hallmarked gold to the local community in Rajasthan, we have grown into the district’s premier luxury showroom.

Under the vision of Sajan Soni, our business has built a reputation for trust and absolute transparency. From simple nose pins to heavy bridal chokers, every transaction at our showroom is backed by correct documentation, computerized carat checking, and honest pricing guidelines.

OUR VISION

To preserve the rich heritage of Rajasthani jewellery art while infusing modern technology and pure hallmarked gold, making JK Jewellers a household name for weddings and investments.

Our Pillars of Luxury

♦

Absolute Purity

We source only government-approved BIS HUID Hallmarked Gold. Every purchase comes with computerized carat checks and authenticity certifications.

Artisanal Heritage

Our designs are handcrafted by hereditary Karigars who specialize in Jadau, Polki, Kundan, and delicate Meenakari artistry.

Ethical Billing

Transparent pricing, exact breakdowns of gold rate, making charges, and stone weights. No hidden charges or billing surprises.

Lifetime Exchange

We offer reliable buyback rates and direct exchange values on our gold and silver collection anytime.
