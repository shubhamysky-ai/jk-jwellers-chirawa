The Indian Bride

Handcrafting Memories For Your Special Day

♦

Wedding jewellery is not just decoration; it is an inheritance. At JK Jewellers, we take pride in being bridal jewellery specialists in Chirawa, Rajasthan. We customize complete wedding packages according to the bride’s styling, weight preferences, and budget coordinates.

Our bridal range features historic designs like the Shekhawati Rajputi Aad, heavy Kundan necklaces, detailed Polki sets with enamel drops, and matching royal maang tikkas, bajuband, and haathphools.

  • Royal Gold Jadau: Thick gold casting decorated with rubies and emerald gems.
  • Polki & Gemstones: Uncut raw gemstones detailed in beautiful frames for royal visual aesthetics.
  • Gold Haar Sets: Detailed royal neckpieces matching modern style.
